        Why Are SEO Necessary

        How SEO Work for Online Visibility

        SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is the process of enhancing a website’s visibility in search engine results. It involves various strategies aimed at improving rankings on search engine results pages (SERPs) for specific keywords relevant to the website’s content. This includes optimizing website structure, content creation, keyword research, backlink building, and technical enhancements. SEO aims to increase organic (non-paid) traffic to a website, ultimately leading to higher visibility, more visitors, and potential conversions or sales.
